My Reviews

Minecraft is a fun creative game where you can build and explore. You can also switch through different modes and worlds. I like that you can build almost anything and choose different modes, tame, and explore. My favorite thing is building and testing different things like redstone and command blocks. Redstone is power, cable and sensory type blocks. Command blocks can power and build things, teleport entities, and summon and destroy things and entities.
